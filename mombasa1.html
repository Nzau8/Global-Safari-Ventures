Mombasa

Top Attractions

  • Fort Jesus
  • Diani Beach
  • Haller Park

Reasons to Visit

  • Relax on pristine white-sand beaches
  • Immerse yourself in Swahili culture and history
  • Explore diverse wildlife and marine parks
